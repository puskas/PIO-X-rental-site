    Javea

    Javea or Xabia in Spanish is the most beautiful town on the Costa Blanca. If you like little beaches and the Blue Mediterranean Sea it is certainly the place to be. The climate is Mediterranean, meaning sunny hot summers and sunny mild winters. The clean and peaceful blue sea escorts its coast along 25 kilometers of beaches, coves and cliffs.

    Puerto
